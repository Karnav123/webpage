My blogs

Till date, I have participated in three internship programs namely TIGP-IIP 2016 , Ekalavya Summer Internship 2015, and NIT Silchar Summer Fellowship 2014. These internships helped me shape my career and hone my skills in a better way. Thus, I decided to share my internship experiences in the form of public blogs. These blogs got published at Internshala Blog, as given below:

  1. First visit to IIT Bombay: During this internship, I worked under the supervision of Prof. Kannan Moudgalya at FOSSEE.
  2. First visit to Taiwan: During this internship, I worked under the supervision of Dr. Ronald Chang at Academia Sinica, Taiwan.
  3. Second visit to IIT Bombay: During this period, I again worked under the supervision of Prof. Kannan Moudgalya at FOSSEE. However, this internship was no less than a surprise for me.
  4. Time on the Stellarium: My second visit to IIT Bombay was re-published by The Hindu.
